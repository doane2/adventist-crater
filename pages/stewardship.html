Stewardship Department

Stewardship is managing the resources God has entrusted to us – time, talent, and treasures – to serve His mission.

What is Stewardship?

Stewardship in the Seventh-day Adventist Church involves managing all aspects of our lives in a way that honors God. This includes how we handle our time, talents, and treasures. It is a response to God's love and a way to participate in His mission here on earth.

Time

Time is a precious gift from God. Stewarding our time well means prioritizing God’s work in our daily lives, from our spiritual practices to how we serve others.

Talent

God has gifted each of us with unique talents. Stewardship means using these talents to build His kingdom by serving in the church and community, and using our skills to help others grow spiritually.

Treasures

Our financial resources are a means to support God’s work. Stewardship encourages us to give generously and wisely, supporting the mission of the church and advancing God’s kingdom on earth.

Stewardship Activities

Our Stewardship Department is involved in a variety of programs and projects designed to support the church’s mission and provide opportunities for church members to engage in service, giving, and resource management. These include:

  • Community Outreach Programs
  • Church Building Fund Contributions
  • Supporting Local and Global Missions
  • Financial Planning Workshops
  • Environmental Stewardship Projects

Impact of Our Stewardship

Through faithful stewardship, the church has been able to fund various projects that have benefited both the local community and the global mission of the SDA Church. We have seen:

  • The construction and renovation of church buildings
  • Support for international missions and relief programs
  • Growth in community outreach and involvement
  • Personal spiritual growth through giving and service

Ways You Can Get Involved

As a member of Crater SDA Church, you are invited to participate in our stewardship activities and contribute to the ongoing work of God’s mission. Here’s how you can get involved:

  • Volunteer for community service projects
  • Contribute to our building fund
  • Donate to support local and international missions
  • Participate in financial literacy workshops
