Our Mission

The Chaplaincy Department at Crater SDA Church is committed to supporting and guiding students in their spiritual journey, both at school and beyond. We aim to introduce young minds to Christ and nurture them in faith through outreach, education, and support in schools.

High School Outreach

Our Chaplaincy team is actively involved in various high schools, providing spiritual guidance, organizing Bible studies, and conducting special events. These initiatives aim to strengthen the students' connection with God and encourage them to make Christ-centered decisions.

  • Bible studies and prayer sessions every week
  • Annual Spiritual Retreats
  • Support for students' personal struggles and challenges

Primary School Initiatives

We also focus on younger children in primary schools, providing them with age-appropriate spiritual content, through Bible stories, character-building programs, and interactive lessons.

  • Weekly children's Bible lessons
  • Holiday Bible camps
  • Support for spiritual growth at home and school

College-Level Impact

At the college level, we continue to nurture the faith of students, preparing them for life beyond their studies with strong spiritual foundations. We hold regular Bible study sessions, outreach programs, and mentorship for students pursuing their career paths.

  • Weekly Bible study groups
  • Campus outreach programs
  • One-on-one mentorship for spiritual growth

Special Projects

Our department is involved in various projects aimed at providing necessary resources for students. We provide Bibles, devotionals, study materials, and even assist in organizing school events that incorporate spiritual elements.

  • Providing free Bibles and devotional books
  • Organizing school-wide religious events and activities
  • Conducting special prayers and worship services at school
