Family Life Department

Building strong, Christ-centered families that reflect the love of God and are a light to the world.

Our Mission

The Family Life Department at Crater SDA Church is dedicated to helping families grow together in love, faith, and commitment. We provide resources, counseling, and guidance on biblical principles of relationships, marriage, and family life. Our goal is to support individuals and couples in building strong, Christ-centered homes that can serve as a witness to the community.

Biblical Teachings on Family Life

The Seventh-day Adventist Church holds a high view of the family, seeing it as a divine institution designed by God. Below are the core teachings on family life based on the churchâ€™s principles.

Relationships

Godâ€™s plan for relationships is centered on love, respect, and faithfulness. The Bible provides guidance on how to build strong relationships based on mutual respect and trust.

Marriage

Marriage is a sacred covenant between a man and a woman, established by God. The Adventist Church teaches that a strong marriage is built on love, mutual respect, commitment, and a shared faith in Christ.

Courtship and Dating

The church encourages young people to approach courtship and dating with seriousness and respect. The goal of courtship is to prepare for marriage, and it should be done in a manner that honors God and respects the other person.

Family Roles

Families are meant to work together as a team, with each member having specific roles that contribute to the unity and harmony of the home. The Bible teaches about the roles of husbands, wives, and children within the family structure.
