Welfare Ministry

Serving our community with compassion and love

About Welfare Ministry

At Crater SDA Church, our Welfare Ministry is dedicated to supporting the well-being of our members and the community. Guided by compassion, empathy, and a commitment to service, we aim to meet physical, emotional, and spiritual needs.

Our Services

Community Support

  • Food drives and clothing donations
  • Counseling services
  • Visitation and prayer for the sick and bereaved

Emergency Assistance

  • Financial aid for urgent needs
  • Disaster relief collaborations

Get Involved

Join us in making a difference. You can support the Welfare Ministry by volunteering, donating resources, or partnering with us to extend our reach.

  • Volunteer Opportunities: Share your time and skills.
  • Donations: Contribute financially or with goods.
  • Partnerships: Collaborate with us for community impact.

Success Stories

Witness how lives have been transformed through the efforts of our Welfare Ministry. Together, we can continue to impact our community for the better.
