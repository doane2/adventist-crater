Children Sabbath Schools

We offer 10 classes from birth through high school each Sabbath at 10:30 am. Every child who attends Nursery through Cradle Roll II must be accompanied by an adult during the program. In Kindergarten I through Primary II, an adult must sign each child in when arriving at Sabbath School and must return to the room to pick the child up.
