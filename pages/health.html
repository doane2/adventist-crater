Health Ministry

The Seventh-day Adventist Church promotes a holistic approach to health, emphasizing physical, mental, and spiritual well-being.

The Health Message

Health is not only about living a long life but living a life that is free from preventable diseases, full of vitality, and closely connected to our relationship with God. The Seventh-day Adventist Church is dedicated to promoting a healthy lifestyle through education and practical health principles rooted in scripture.

NEWSTART Program

The NEWSTART program emphasizes the 8 key principles that contribute to optimal health and well-being:

  • Nutrition
  • Exercise
  • Water
  • Sunshine
  • Temperance
  • Air
  • Rest
  • Trust in God

Biblical Health Principles

The Bible teaches us the importance of taking care of our bodies, as they are temples of the Holy Spirit (1 Corinthians 6:19-20). Proper health is not just a physical matter but also involves spiritual, mental, and emotional well-being. We believe that God desires His children to live full, healthy lives that reflect His glory.

Health Programs

Our Health Ministry runs various programs aimed at improving the health of our community. These programs include:

  • Health Screenings (blood pressure, diabetes, etc.)
  • Health Seminars on diet, exercise, and mental health
  • Cooking Classes for healthy plant-based meals
  • Health Fairs providing free consultations and resources
  • Support for addiction recovery and mental health programs

Impact of Health Ministry

Through our health programs, we have seen positive changes in the physical and spiritual lives of our community. Some of the key impacts include:

  • Improved health awareness and prevention of chronic diseases
  • Better lifestyle choices made by participants in health seminars
  • Stronger bonds in the church and community through health initiatives
  • More people committing to a lifestyle of wellness, fitness, and faith

Get Involved

We invite you to join us in our mission to promote holistic health. Here’s how you can get involved:

  • Volunteer for health events and wellness fairs
  • Join our health education seminars and cooking classes
  • Contribute financially to support our programs and outreach
