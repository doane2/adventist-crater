Children's Ministry

Nurturing the faith and spiritual growth of our children, preparing them to serve God and others.

Our Mission

The Children’s Ministry at Crater SDA Church is dedicated to guiding children in their faith journey, encouraging them to develop a deep relationship with Jesus Christ while fostering a supportive, engaging, and nurturing environment. We offer a variety of classes for children of all ages, based on the teachings of the Seventh-day Adventist Church manual, to ensure they grow spiritually and emotionally.

Cradle Roll (Birth to 3 Years)

The Cradle Roll class introduces the youngest children to Jesus in a fun and engaging way, teaching them about God's love through simple Bible stories and songs. The goal is to lay a foundation of faith early in life.

Kindergarten (Ages 3-6)

Kindergarten children learn more about Jesus through Bible stories, music, and hands-on activities. They are taught the basics of the Christian faith in a fun and interactive manner to make learning engaging.

Primary (Ages 6-9)

Primary children begin to learn deeper Bible stories and are encouraged to apply Christian principles in their daily lives. They also start memorizing Bible verses and understand the importance of prayer and worship.

Juniors (Ages 9-12)

In the Junior class, children continue to build a personal relationship with God through prayer, Bible study, and service. They are taught how to live as Christ's ambassadors in their homes, school, and community.

Earliteen (Ages 12-14)

The Earliteen class focuses on preparing pre-teens for adolescence and adulthood. They are guided to understand God's plan for their lives and how they can make positive, faith-driven decisions in the world around them.

Pathfinders Program

The Pathfinders program is designed to teach children valuable life skills, outdoor activities, and Christian principles while fostering a love for God. The Pathfinders is organized in various levels based on the age of the participants, with each level focusing on specific honors and achievements.

Friend (Ages 10-12)

  • Honors: Knot Tying, Nature Study, First Aid, Camping
  • Activities: Camping trips, community service projects, Bible study

Companion (Ages 12-14)

  • Honors: Archery, Hiking, Wilderness Survival, Cooking
  • Activities: Leadership training, advanced outdoor skills

Explorer (Ages 14-16)

  • Honors: Orienteering, Photography, Outdoor Skills
  • Activities: Mission trips, leadership retreats, special service projects

Adventurers Program

The Adventurers program is for younger children, ages 6-9, and focuses on teaching foundational Christian principles through hands-on activities and experiences. Children are encouraged to explore their faith and develop a love for service to others.

Classes in Adventurers

  • Busy Bee (Ages 6-7)
  • Sunbeam (Ages 7-8)
  • Builder (Ages 8-9)
  • Helping Hand (Ages 8-9)

Honors for Adventurers

  • Health & Fitness
  • First Aid
  • Hiking
  • Arts & Crafts
  • Cooking
  • Music
  • Nature Study
